lasagna recipe

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

ingridients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage.
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef.
  • ½ cup minced onion.
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed.

steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  4. Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat those layers two times.
